BREAKDOWN DESCRIPTION:
  At approximately 10:45, CNC Machine M-12 tripped mid-cycle during machining
  of shaft blank (batch SHF-NOV-018). Control panel displayed fault code E-047
  (Spindle Overload). Machine halted automatically. Operator Ramesh Pillai
  stopped the job and informed Rajesh Sharma immediately.

  Spindle was found hot to touch. Coolant flow to spindle appeared reduced.
  Coolant reservoir level found at 30% — below the 50% minimum operating level
  marked on the tank.

TIMELINE:
  10:45 — Breakdown occurred. Fault code E-047 displayed.
  10:50 — Rajesh Sharma arrived at machine.
  11:00 — Initial inspection done. Coolant level low identified as probable cause.
  11:20 — Coolant (Part No. COOL-SOL-07, 1:20 dilution) topped up (12 Ltrs added).
           Spindle allowed to cool — 30 minutes rest.
  11:55 — Machine restarted. Trial run — no fault. Spindle temp normal.
  12:00 — Machine returned to production.

PROBABLE CAUSE:
  Insufficient coolant supply leading to spindle thermal overload.
  Coolant consumption appears higher than usual — last top-up was 25-Oct-2024
  (approx. 14 days ago, 10 Ltrs added at that time). Normal interval is 21–28
  days for this machine.

PARTS / MATERIALS USED:
  COOL-SOL-07 (Water-Soluble Coolant Concentrate): 12 Ltrs
  Issue Slip : IS-2024-1163

DOWNTIME:
  Duration          : 1 hour 15 minutes (10:45 – 12:00)
  Production Impact : 4 shaft blanks lost (batch SHF-NOV-018 delayed)

CORRECTIVE ACTIONS:
1. Coolant topped up and concentration verified (refractometer check: 5.2% — OK).
2. Coolant reservoir level to be added to daily operator checklist for M-12.
3. Check coolant lines and fittings for possible micro-leaks — scheduled for
   next available slot (target: 11-Nov-2024).
4. Spindle motor temperature to be monitored for next 3 shifts.

FURTHER MONITORING REQUIRED: YES
  — Coolant consumption trend to be tracked.
