DESCRIPTION OF NON-CONFORMANCE:
  During routine QA inspection of batch PL2-NOV-042, surface finish (Ra)
  parameter was found out of specification in 4 of 10 sampled units.

  Specified limit : Ra ≤ 3.2 µm
  Measured values : 3.4 µm, 3.5 µm, 3.6 µm, 3.4 µm (4 units)
  Remaining 6 units : within specification (2.5 – 3.1 µm)

  Additionally, 2 units showed minor flatness deviation on mating face:
  Specified limit : ≤ 0.05 mm
  Measured values : 0.06 mm, 0.08 mm

  This represents a worsening trend from batch PL2-NOV-041 (QIR-2024-0391)
  where 2 of 10 samples were marginal on surface finish and 1 was marginal on
  flatness. The trend is escalating.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
POTENTIAL CAUSE (Initial Assessment):
  Surface finish and flatness deviations on pressed components are typically
  influenced by:
  a) Pressing pressure consistency — HP-05 experienced pressure fluctuation on
     04-Nov-2024 (MWO-2024-0843). Although pressure was corrected on 05-Nov,
     the root cause of valve drift was not formally confirmed (ref: internal
     email 06-Nov-2024, Rajesh Sharma to K. Narayanan).
  b) Tooling wear — pressing die condition not recently inspected.
  c) Raw material variation — casting blank quality from current lot.

  Formal root cause analysis not yet completed. Immediate containment initiated.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IMMEDIATE CONTAINMENT:
  - Batch PL2-NOV-042: 120 units placed ON HOLD pending 100% inspection.
  - 100% inspection of surface finish and flatness to be completed by
    Vikram Singh and one additional QA inspector by 09-Nov-2024.
  - Production of batch PL2-NOV-043 to continue but with enhanced in-process
    checks: surface finish to be verified every 20 units (vs. routine end-of-
    batch sampling).
